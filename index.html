What are Weather Prediction Markets?

Prediction markets (like Kalshi) are binary options platforms where users bet on the outcome of future events. They offer a unique environment where retail emotion often overwhelms statistical probability.

In weather markets specifically, the general public trades based on localized observation and headline news, creating a massive divergence between perceived likelihood and actual meteorological & statistical probabilities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to leave my computer on?

Yes. ARCHER runs locally on your own hardware. The system needs to be active during your configured trading windows to monitor markets and execute positions.

What technical requirements are there?

ARCHER is designed to run on any modern desktop or laptop. A stable internet connection is required. Detailed system requirements will be shared ahead of release.

What markets are supported?

The initial release targets weather prediction markets on Kalshi exclusively. Additional markets and exchanges are planned for subsequent releases.

Is my capital secure?

ARCHER does not hold your funds. You maintain full custody of your capital in your Kalshi account; the system only requires API keys to place orders on your behalf.

How much capital do I need?

There is no enforced minimum. However, we recommend starting with at least $1,000 to allow meaningful position sizing within Kalshi's contract limits.

When will ARCHER be available?

We are targeting a first release in Q4 2026. Waitlist members will be notified by email as soon as access opens.

Can I customise the trading strategy?

Yes. ARCHER exposes all core parameters — risk per trade, execution windows, market filters, and bias thresholds — through a local configuration file you control entirely.

Is there a subscription fee?

Pricing details will be announced closer to launch. Waitlist members will receive priority access and early-adopter terms before public pricing goes live.

What happens to my data?

Your trading activity stays on your machine. We only collect the contact details you submit here, used solely to notify you at launch and in accordance with our Privacy Policy.
